NUMBER CHASER

In the "Number Chaser" game, five numbers will briefly appear on your screen before disappearing. Your challenge is to find the correct answer by either adding or subtracting the numbers. You have three attempts to get the answer right, with three difficulty levels to choose from: Easy, Medium, and Hard. You can select between addition and subtraction for solving the problems. This game is not only fun but also educational, making it a great choice for kids to enjoy while learning.
Technologies Used: Android Studio, JavaScript, Kotlin

10 SECONDS GAME

In the "10 Seconds" game, pressing the start button will begin a timer but you can't see the time, and your goal is to stop the timer exactly at 10 seconds by pressing the stop button. You have unlimited chances to get it right, and you can play this game with your friends for fun. Compete to see who can stop the timer closest to the 10-second mark or challenge each other to improve your timing skills. It’s a simple yet engaging game that tests your precision and timing under pressure.
Technologies Used: Android Studio, JavaScript, Kotlin
